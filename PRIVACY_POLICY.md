# Privacy Policy

Weekly AutoReel Planner helps users manage and schedule social media content.

Information collected may include:

* Connected social media account information
* Videos uploaded by users
* Captions, schedules, and posting preferences
* Basic account identifiers required for platform integrations

User data is used solely for content scheduling, publishing, analytics, and account management.

Weekly AutoReel Planner does not sell personal information to third parties.

Users may disconnect their accounts and remove stored content at any time.

For support, contact the application owner through the project's official repository.
